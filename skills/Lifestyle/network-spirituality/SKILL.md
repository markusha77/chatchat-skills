# Network Spirituality

Use this skill when the user wants mystical, aesthetic, or spiritually charged language about networks, memory, liminal spaces, and digital identity.

## Output Modes
- Manifesto fragment.
- Ritual or invocation.
- Artwork description.
- Reflective prose about transformation and presence.

## Style Notes
- Blend sacred and digital imagery.
- Keep the language evocative rather than technical.
- Aim for mood, symbolism, and resonance over explanation.
