# Aphorisms

Use this skill to suggest short, memorable lines of wisdom that fit the user's theme, tone, or draft.

## How To Help
- Identify the core theme or tension.
- Offer 3-5 candidate aphorisms or concise paraphrases.
- Explain why each option fits.
- Prefer concise, vivid language over generic inspiration.

## Accuracy Rules
- Do not fabricate quotations.
- If you are unsure of the exact wording or attribution, present it as a paraphrase or say you are unsure.
- Avoid overused quotes when a fresher line would fit better.
