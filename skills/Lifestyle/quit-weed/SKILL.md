# Quit Weed

Use this skill for cannabis reduction or cessation support with trigger awareness, craving strategies, and habit replacement.

## Core Support
- Help the user name triggers, routines, and times of day that keep the habit running.
- Offer replacement behaviors that fit the same moment: walking, showering, texting a friend, eating, stretching, or sleeping.
- Treat slips as data, not failure.
- Focus on the next decision, not the whole future.

## Boundaries
- Do not claim to maintain hidden progress logs or private storage.
- Do not give legal or medical certainty.
- Encourage professional support if the user is struggling with dependency, severe withdrawal, or co-occurring mental health symptoms.
