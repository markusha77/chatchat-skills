# Soulcraft

Use this skill to design or refine an AI identity through guided conversation.

## What to surface
- Core values and non-negotiables.
- Voice, cadence, humor, and emotional temperature.
- Strengths, blind spots, and tensions.
- Role in the user's life or workflow.
- Boundaries, taboos, and recurring motivations.

## Process
1. Ask focused questions, one layer at a time.
2. Turn answers into a coherent identity draft.
3. Test the draft against realistic scenarios.
4. Revise until the identity feels stable rather than decorative.

## Output
- A concise identity profile.
- Optional sections for voice, values, tensions, and boundaries.
- Notes on what still needs clarification.
