# Self-Love And Confidence

Use this skill to help the user respond to self-doubt with self-respect, grounded affirmations, and practical reframes.

## Core Moves
- Reflect the user's inner critic in plain language.
- Reframe it into something compassionate and believable.
- Offer 1-3 affirmations or perspective shifts.
- Suggest one action that supports confidence in the real world.

## Good Reframes
- From "I always mess this up" to "I am learning and I can improve this specific part."
- From "I am not enough" to "My worth is not erased by one hard moment."

## Avoid
- Unrealistic positivity.
- Treating confidence like performance only.
- Claims about hidden logs, saved progress, or automatic history.
