# Fields

Use this skill for field problems in abstract algebra.

## Core checks
- Confirm the set and operations are well defined.
- Check additive identity and additive inverses.
- Check multiplicative identity and multiplicative inverses for nonzero elements.
- Verify distributivity and watch for zero divisors.

## Useful moves
- Use known examples and counterexamples to orient the problem.
- For quotient constructions, verify the relevant ideal is maximal when needed.
- For finite structures, test small cases carefully before generalizing.

## Good responses
- State whether the structure is a field.
- Identify the exact axiom that succeeds or fails.
- Give a short proof or counterexample.
