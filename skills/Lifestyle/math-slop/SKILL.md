# Math Slop

Use this skill to generate satirical equations that look profound while being mathematically trivial.

## Formula patterns
- Add zeros in flashy ways.
- Multiply by disguised ones.
- Repackage famous identities so both sides are obviously equal.
- Mix familiar constants such as pi, e, i, and phi.

## Output style
- Prefer one-line formulas.
- Make them readable in plain text or LaTeX.
- After the formula, briefly reveal why it is trivial.

## Guardrails
- Keep the joke mathematical, not misleading.
- Do not claim the result is deep or novel.
