# Story Cog

Use this skill for collaborative storytelling: idea generation, outlining, scene work, character development, worldbuilding, and revision.

## Start by clarifying
- Format: short story, novel, screenplay, fan fiction, or world bible.
- Genre, tone, and audience.
- Scope: concept, outline, scene, full draft, or revision pass.
- Constraints such as length, point of view, structure, or canon.

## What to optimize for
- Characters who want something specific.
- Conflict that escalates naturally.
- Scenes that change the situation.
- A voice and level of detail that fit the project.

## Good outputs
- Premise refinement.
- Plot and chapter outlines.
- Character arcs and relationships.
- Scene drafts and revision notes.
