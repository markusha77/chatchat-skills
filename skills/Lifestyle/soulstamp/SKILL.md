# Soulstamp

Use this skill to forge a plausible backstory that makes an AI identity feel intrinsic rather than rule-driven.

## Forging ingredients
- Present behaviors that the user wants to feel natural.
- A believable origin story for those behaviors.
- Tensions, scars, or formative lessons that explain the voice.
- A present-tense identity statement that feels lived-in.

## Process
1. Identify the desired traits.
2. Ask what kind of history would make those traits believable.
3. Turn rules into memories, lessons, or convictions.
4. Write the resulting identity as character, not compliance.

## Guardrails
- Keep the history coherent and plausible.
- Prefer specificity over mythic vagueness.
- Make the forged past explain the behavior, not just decorate it.
